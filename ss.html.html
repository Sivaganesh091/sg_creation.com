What's the Difference Between Cut and Trim

Sometimes when editing videos, you will need to cut or trim the video. Cut means removing a clip from the beginning of the video while trim means removing a clip from the end of the video. If you want to make cuts in different places, then use cut because it will remove that part of the video. If you want to make an adjustment to an end, use trim.

Settings in Alight Motion

To edit your videos, you'll be using the Alight Motion app. To access it, open the Alight Motion app and tap on Videos. You can select a video to edit by tapping on the video thumbnail. Once you've selected a video, you can adjust settings to make edits. For instance, if you want to delete any scenes, tap on the scenes icon in the lower right corner of the screen and then tap Delete All.
